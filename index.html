Welcome

A family tradition of raising cattle the right way

Summers Ranch is a family-owned Hereford cattle operation on 160 acres of rolling pasture in Sutter Creek, California. We raise and carefully manage our herd, tracking lineage and health records to produce strong, quality animals for our buyers. Whether you're looking for breeding stock or sale cattle, we invite you to learn more about our operation and the care we put into every animal.
