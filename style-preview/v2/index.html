Burgundy Accent
Closest to the original Weathered. Cream and ranger-green stay intact; burgundy replaces bronze for kickers, accents, and buttons. A conservative integration — the ranch's shirt-palette color joins the system without reshaping it.

Style 4 · Burgundy variant
Merlot & Cream
The heaviest commitment to the ranch's shirt palette. Burgundy and cream carry the entire design together — other colors step back. More branded, more professional-catalog, less "dusty old ranch." In dark mode, the shirt palette's black-and-burgundy version comes through directly.
Display: Playfair Display Body: Work Sans Burgundy role: Core brand (with cream)
