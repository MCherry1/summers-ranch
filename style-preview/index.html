Read me first

This is a choose-your-trim-options page. The site we're building will have the same structure, features, and content regardless — what you're picking is how it looks and feels.

Each of the four panels below shows the same content in a different style: a home-page hero with your ranch's name, a short paragraph, a "See the Herd" button, an example cow card, and typography samples. The photos and cow details are the same across all four so you can compare directly.

You don't need to analyze these. Scroll through, see which one feels right, and tell Matt. If two feel right and one feels wrong, that's useful too. If none feel right, that's also useful — we can do another round.

What to look for: Does the ranch's name feel dignified? Does the tone match how you'd want buyers to perceive Summers Ranch — professional, welcoming, serious, warm? Does the cow card feel trustworthy?

Each panel takes about 30 seconds to scan. The whole page takes under 5 minutes total.
